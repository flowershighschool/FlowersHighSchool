Meknes Directorate: Recognizing Achievements of National Olympiad Participants

Dr. Wafa Chaker, the Regional Director in Meknes, warmly welcomed the participants of the National Olympiad training held at the School of Industrial Management of the Mohammed VI University in Benguerir on Thursday, April 7. These students, who represented the Regional Directorate in mathematics, biology, chemistry, and informatics subjects, achieved outstanding results in the exercises conducted over four days from April 3 to 6, 2022. The training yielded very advanced and encouraging outcomes, with one student, Hiba Kasimi qualifying for the African Olympiad in Mathematics.

Financial Literacy Training at Bank Al Maghrib Benefits Flowers High School Students

On March 21, 2021, Flowers High School students benefited from a financial literacy training session at Bank Al Maghrib. The session covered topics such as identifying counterfeit currency and understanding the role of checks in financial transactions. The training empowered the students with essential financial literacy skills, preparing them for responsible financial decision-making in the future. Flowers High School is grateful for the collaboration with Bank Al Maghrib in promoting financial awareness among its students and equipping them with valuable skills for managing their finances effectively.
